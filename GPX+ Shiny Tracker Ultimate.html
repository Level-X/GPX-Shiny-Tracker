Information on the Ultimate Edition
  • This version contains every Pokédex entry on Global PokédeX Plus and all gender differences.
  • To mark a Pokémon as "owned" you can use the JavaScript feature by left-clicking on its icon.
  • Hover over icons to see their Pokédex number, species name, current form and typing.
  • National Pokédex order takes priority in the sorting of entries, including Novelty Pokémon.
  • Pokémon that haven't been released use placeholder data, which will be updated in the future.

Stuck and need some help? Check out the Getting Started page!
